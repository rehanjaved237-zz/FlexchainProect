WELCOME TO FLEXCHAIN

WHAT IS FLEXCHAIN ?

FlexChain is a custom built permissioned blockchain which will allow our university to manage its students grades and marks within a decentralized blockchain.

SECURE

No Single Point of Failure as the system is decentralized, using cryptographic algorithms to validate the blockchain makes it much more secure than the traditional centralized database systems.

IMMUTABLE LEDGER

Once a record is entered into the blockchain, it cannot be deleted, removed or even altered.

DECENTRALIZED

Each component of the system is equally responsible for contributing to the global, complex behavior by acting on local information in the appropriate manner

HOW TO USE FLEXCHAIN

STUDENT

Using FlexChain, Students would be able to view their marks and the final grade assigned by the instructor in a particular course.

TEACHER

Using FlexChain, Teachers would be able to manage students in an assigned particular course.

HEAD OF DEPARTMENT

Using FlexChain, Head of Department would be able to offer courses for a particular semester and validate the grades assigned by the instructor in a particular course.

FLEXCHAIN AT BACKEND

BLOCKS

Blocks are the fundamental units of a blockchain which contains records (data). In FlexChain a block is assigned to a course which contains student name and its respective grade in the course assigned by the teacher. Once a block is made, it is broadcasted into the network and a miner is selected via consensus algorithm which then verifies it.

MINING

Mining is the process of adding records to Blockchain's public ledger of past transactions (data). In FlexChain Miner will be the head of department who will mine the block proposed by the instructors, the miner will verify the given block via comparing hashes and add to the blockchain in case verified. There will be a total of 3-4 Miners in FlexChain as there are 3-4 HOD's

UPDATE BLOCKCHAIN

After Mining, the blockchain will be updated by the head of department and the blockchain will be broadcasted to all the network to build upon new blocks on blockchain. As FlexChain Users will be few in numbers considering Instuctors and Head of Departments only thus blockchain will be broadcasted in less time and FlexChain would be consistent throughout.
